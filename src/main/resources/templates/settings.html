Why we ask this
Personality data is used only to rank your match results — it's never shared with other users or third parties. Your raw quiz answers, personality vector, and descriptors are stored privately on your account and deleted the moment you choose to remove them. Matching works by comparing your profile to others using a similarity score; no one sees your individual trait values.
