01
Take the BFI quiz
BFI (Big Five Inventory) is a research-backed personality framework. A short 15-question version maps you across 5 dimensions — curiosity, warmth, energy, focus, and sensitivity. This shapes every match you get.
02
Get matched by vibe
AI finds locals whose personality profile overlaps yours. Each result shows exactly why you'd click — not just where they live.

Both check in when it's time. Explore the city together. Afterwards, a Memory Card is generated — a permanent record of your meet.
